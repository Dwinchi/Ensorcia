Basic Spells

Earth Manipulation

[This spell allows the user to control rocks and dirt.]

Req: Lots of stamina, Body Strength (Dependent on how big the chunk of earth is)

Plant Growth Influence

[This spell allows the user to grow plants.]

Req: Lots of stamina, Mental Strength, Proper Spell Training

Medicine Creation

[This allows the user to put special magic into herbal medicine.]

Req: Lots of stamina, Mental and Body Strength, Knowledge of medicinal ingredients, Proper Spell Training

Advanced Spells

Plant Manipulation

[This allows the user to control plants around them.]

Req: Lots of Stamina, Mental and Body Strength, Peace of Mind, Focus, Proper Spell Training, Strong Connection to Nature

Self Healing

[This allows the user to heal themselves.]

Req: Lots of Stamina, Mental and Body Strength, Peace of Mind, Focus, Proper Spell Training, Strong Connection to Nature, 2 Years of training

Tree of Life

[This allows the user to heal multiple at a time others at a time, or simply just one. Healing slows with the number of people]

Req: Lots of Stamina, Mental and Body Strength, Peace of Mind, Focus, Proper Spell Training, Strong Connection to Nature, 5 years of training

Earth Clones

[This allows the user to create 5 clones of themselves from dirt/mud.]

Req: Lots of Stamina, Mental and Body Strength, Peace of Mind, Focus, Proper Spell Training, Strong Connection to Nature, 7 years of training
